How does it work?

DropFinder is a specialized program designed to identify and locate beat drops within a song by analyzing its audio characteristics.

The program picks out the loudest parts from the song's first half, and then does the same for the second half. This mirrors the usual setup of songs, where you'll often find two distinct drops or energetic moments.
